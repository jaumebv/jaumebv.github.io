Katto: Rising Tides

Steam Release & GDWC Finalist

Overview

Katto: Rising Tides is a fun and dynamic 3D platformer developed in Unreal Engine 5 as part of a collaborative project by Arcadia Games. The game features smooth platforming mechanics, fluid controls, stylised visuals and a lively atmosphere, all designed to create a cohesive and enjoyable experience.


  • Platform: PC (Controller support)
  • Release date: July 4th, 2024
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 5
  • Team: 7 Programmers · 2 Designers · 7 Artists · 5 Musicians
  • Development time: ~7 months

My Work

Collaboration & Responsibilities

Collaborated closely with 7 programmers, 2 designers, and 8 artists, ensuring smooth communication between all departments to maintain visual and gameplay consistency throughout the development process.

Custom Level Streaming

Designed and implemented a custom Level Streaming system in Unreal Engine, allowing designers to dynamically load and unload game areas in real time. This improved performance and flexibility during both development and gameplay.

Enemy Logic & AI

Created the AI and combat behaviour for two of the main enemies, integrating animations from the art team and ensuring smooth transitions between idle, attack, and reaction states. Focused on responsiveness and animation timing to make combat feel polished and reactive.
